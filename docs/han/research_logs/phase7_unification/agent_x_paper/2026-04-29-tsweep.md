## Phase 7 T-sweep — barebones TXCDR + hill-climbed H8 multidistance, 2-seed σ, BALANCED-15

> Phase 7 paper artefact (ii). Both T-sweep families at every-T cell with
> two seeds (1, 42); FLIP-corrected; S=32 mean-pool aggregation.
> **Task set: BALANCED-15** (paper headline reduction; rationale in
> `2026-04-29-task-importance.md`).

### Plot

![T-sweep](plots/phase7_tsweep_2seed.png)

### Data

- 15 SAEBench tasks (BALANCED reduction of the 36-task standard set).
- S = 32 left-aligned cache, mean-pool aggregation.
- Seed=1 + seed=42; per-cell `n_seeds=2` for every entry below.
- Per-cell metric: cross-seed mean of per-task means.
- σ_seeds: std across the two per-seed means.

Code: `experiments/phase7_unification/build_tsweep_2seed.py` (filters
to `BALANCED_15` from `experiments/phase7_unification/task_sets.py`).

### Shape and headline takeaways (BALANCED-15)

- Both families are **non-monotonic in T**: peak around T=4-9, gradual
  decline through T=10-30, slight recovery at T=32 for barebones.
- At k_feat=5 the hill-climbed H8 family is **slightly above**
  barebones TXCDR at the same T (e.g., t8: hillclimb 0.8641 vs
  barebones 0.8549; t9: 0.8601 vs 0.8555; t4: 0.8614 vs 0.8607).
  Differences are within σ_seeds (~0.005-0.013).
- At k_feat=20 the two families **overlap closely** (within σ_seeds);
  hill-climbed is no longer clearly above barebones once the LR has
  20-feature budget.
- σ_seeds is small everywhere (0.0006-0.0138 — slightly larger than
  the full-36 case because each per-T value is averaged over fewer
  tasks, giving more per-seed variance), so the within-family T
  trend is robust to seed.
- Both families' k=5 winner is at T=4-9 — supporting the view that
  small-T temporal aggregation helps probing AUC at very-sparse
  budgets, while T ≥ 16 is in the "sparsity-collapse" regime. Same
  qualitative shape as full-36 T-sweep.

### Cross-task-set sanity check

Comparing best-T peaks across full-36 vs BALANCED-15 (k_feat=5):

| family | full-36 best T | full-36 mean | BALANCED-15 best T | BALANCED-15 mean |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| barebones | T=7 | 0.8952 | T=4 | 0.8607 |
| hill-climbed H8 | T=4 | 0.8957 | T=8 | 0.8641 |

Different peak-T cells, but the qualitative shape (peak at small T,
decline at high T) holds in both. Absolute AUCs drop ~0.03-0.04 in
BALANCED-15 because we dropped some easy near-ceiling tasks.
